- **Chinese mirror:** `SKILL.zh-CN.md` + `references/*.zh-CN.md` (same behavior; page language still follows the user—see `SKILL.md`).

- **Hosts:** Cursor, Claude Code, Windsurf, OpenClaw — paths in `SKILL.md` / `SKILL.zh-CN.md`.
